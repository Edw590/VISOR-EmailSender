Means, I want to get a kind of 'tablet experience' on my phone, having very small icons and elements, but getting a lot of screen esitate for overview like on a desktop-PC. Reult shall look similar to this" https://images.app.goo.gl/42S35zV6MbftPGjr5 So I did rooting on all my 'old' phone right after purchase (taking the risk to void the warranty), to get this result. However, for BV9500 is was not necessary to root, because I can get this result also without root, just 'developer status' enabled. I need also to say, that I never take care about the 'cost' of a phone...means I always want the 'BEST' I can get, and dont look at the price tag. Some of my previous phones where >1,5k EUR. In case of the BV9500 it is the strange situation that the best phone is at the same time sooo cheap...thats crazy but true ! Like a new Porsche for 15k :-) This low price misleads a lot of people that this device is crap...but it's not the case at all...its just opposite. Frankly: I would even have bought it if it would be 1.200EUR (not joking!), because the sum of all features together is sooo uniqe and convincing:-) Have a nice time & a lot of fun !
